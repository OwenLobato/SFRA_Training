Feature Degradation Impacting Einstein Bots

Fecha: 08:50 pm CEST, July 12

Estado: Closed

Issue: Salesforce is investigating a feature degradation with Einstein Bots.

Impact: A subset of customers may experience errors while using Einstein Bots. Bots are intermittently not responding, and the conversations may not be redirected to a live person.

On July 12, 2023 at 20:02 UTC; the Salesforce Technology team performed a restart to restore the services. We apologize for any impact this had on you and your organization. The team will conduct a full investigation of this issue.
